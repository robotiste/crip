CIC Robotics Internship Program

CRIP is an initiative taken by AUTONOMI, the Robotics Society of Cluster Innovation Centre, University of Delhi.
We offer an opportunity for students to work on a 2 month-long summer internship under the mentorship of experienced Roboticists with guidance of expert Professors, Industrialists and Researchers.
